The City Tour Cusco is an excellent option for those who want to discover the main historical and cultural attractions of the city in a short time. This tour begins with a visit to the Qoricancha Temple, one of the most important ceremonial centers of the Inca Empire, where the god Inti (the Sun) was worshipped.

Next, the tour continues to the impressive Sacsayhuaman fortress, known for its enormous perfectly fitted stone blocks. From this vantage point, panoramic views of the city of Cusco can also be enjoyed.

Three important archaeological complexes are then visited: Q'enqo, a ceremonial center; Puka Pukara, an ancient military fortress; and Tambomachay, known as the Inca's baths, associated with the cult of water. Each of these sites has unique characteristics that allow for a deeper understanding of the organization and spirituality of the Andean world.

Finally, the tour concludes with the return to the city of Cusco. This experience combines history, culture and landscapes, making it ideal as an introduction to the Inca legacy before visiting other destinations such as Machu Picchu.
