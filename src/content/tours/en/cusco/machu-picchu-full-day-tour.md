The Machu Picchu Full Day Tour is one of the most complete experiences for discovering one of the wonders of the world in a single day. The day begins with a hotel pickup in Cusco, followed by a transfer to Ollantaytambo, where the tourist train departs for Aguas Calientes.

During the train journey, spectacular scenery of mountains, rivers and Andean valleys can be admired. Upon arrival, a professional guide will accompany you to the Consettur bus station, from where you ascend to the citadel of Machu Picchu in approximately 30 minutes.

At Machu Picchu, a guided tour of approximately 2 hours is carried out, visiting the main sections of the sanctuary, such as temples, agricultural terraces and viewpoints. The guide will share the history, significance and mystery of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

After the visit, you return to Aguas Calientes to enjoy lunch and free time. You then board the return train to Ollantaytambo and continue the journey by transport to Cusco, ending the tour with a drop-off at the hotel.

This tour is ideal for those who want to experience Machu Picchu comfortably, in an organized manner and in a single day.
