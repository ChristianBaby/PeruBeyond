The Sacred Valley of the Incas Tour is a complete experience that combines history, culture and impressive landscapes in a single day. The tour begins with a hotel pickup in Cusco between 07:00 and 07:50 a.m., before heading to the Sacred Valley.

The first stop is Pisac, where its archaeological complex is visited and its famous agricultural terraces are admired, along with panoramic views of the valley. The tour then continues to Urubamba, where a buffet lunch with typical regional dishes is enjoyed.

In the afternoon, the Ollantaytambo fortress is visited, one of the best-preserved Inca constructions, renowned for its architecture and historical importance. Finally, the tour culminates in Chinchero, where its archaeological park is explored and visitors have the opportunity to learn about traditional Andean weaving techniques.

The tour concludes with the return to Cusco at approximately 7:00 p.m., dropping travelers off near the Plaza de Armas. This experience is ideal for those who want to discover the best of the Sacred Valley in a single day.
