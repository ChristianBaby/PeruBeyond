Live a unique experience in Peru traveling from the coast to the Andes in a 10-day, 9-night trip full of culture, history and adventure. Start in Lima exploring its colonial heritage and modernity, continue to Paracas and Ica with the Ballestas Islands and the Huacachina oasis, and then travel to Cusco to discover the Inca legacy.

Explore the Sacred Valley, visit Machu Picchu and complement the experience with excursions to Humantay Lake, Maras, Moray and Rainbow Mountain. A complete journey that combines nature, culture and adventure in an unforgettable experience.
