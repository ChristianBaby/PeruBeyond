The 7-day Colonial Lima, Ica, Cusco and Machu Picchu tour offers a complete experience that combines history, culture and adventure. It starts in Lima, where the historic center and modern areas are explored, followed by an excursion to Paracas and Ica, where marine wildlife, vineyards and adventure sports in Huacachina are enjoyed.

The journey continues in Cusco, exploring the Sacred Valley of the Incas with its markets and archaeological complexes. Finally, Machu Picchu is visited — one of the wonders of the modern world — where its history and unique architecture are discovered.

An ideal journey for those who wish to see Peru's main attractions in a single trip.
