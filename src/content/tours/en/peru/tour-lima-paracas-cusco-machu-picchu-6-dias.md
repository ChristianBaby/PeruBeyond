The Lima, Paracas, Huacachina and Machu Picchu tour is a complete 6-day experience that combines the best of Peru's coast and highlands. It starts in Lima, where arrival and preparation for the journey take place, followed by an excursion to Paracas and Huacachina, where marine wildlife, vineyards and adventure sports in the desert are enjoyed.

Subsequently, the journey continues to Cusco, where the Andean experience is lived by visiting the Sacred Valley of the Incas, with its landscapes, markets and archaeological complexes. The highlight of the trip is the visit to Machu Picchu, one of the wonders of the modern world, where its history, architecture and cultural significance are explored.

Finally, the tour concludes with the return to Lima, closing an experience that combines nature, culture and adventure in a single unforgettable journey.
