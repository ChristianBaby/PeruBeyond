The 7-day Lima, Paracas, Huacachina, Cusco and Machu Picchu tour offers a complete experience that travels through Peru's coast and highlands. It starts in Lima, followed by an adventure to Paracas and Huacachina, where marine wildlife, vineyards and adventure sports in the desert are combined.

Subsequently, the journey continues in Cusco, where Machu Picchu is visited — one of the wonders of the modern world. In addition, excursions are made to the impressive Humantay Lake and Rainbow Mountain, two of the country's most stunning natural destinations.

This journey combines history, culture, nature and adventure, making it ideal for those who want to discover the best of Peru in a single experience.
