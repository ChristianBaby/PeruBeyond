This 8-day, 7-night Peru tour will allow you to explore the best of the coast, the desert and the Andes in a single trip. It starts in Lima, the historical and gastronomic capital of South America, before experiencing adventure in the deserts of Paracas and Ica, sailing past the Ballestas Islands and enjoying the Huacachina oasis. You will then fly to Cusco, the imperial Inca city, to tour the Sacred Valley, discover the impressive citadel of Machu Picchu, and take unforgettable excursions to the magical Humantay Lake and the stunning Rainbow Mountain (Vinicunca).

An ideal experience for those seeking history, culture, adventure and unique landscapes in a single trip through Peru.
