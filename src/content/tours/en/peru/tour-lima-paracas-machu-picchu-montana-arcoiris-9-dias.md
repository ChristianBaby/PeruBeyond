Discover the best of Peru on a 9-day, 8-night journey that combines the cultural richness of the coast with the majesty of the Andes. This tour begins in Lima, the gastronomic and cultural capital of the country, where you will appreciate its colonial and modern charm. You will then travel to Paracas and Ica to explore the famous Ballestas Islands and experience the thrill of the Huacachina oasis.

You will continue to Cusco, the ancient capital of the Inca Empire, where you will discover temples, cobblestone streets and archaeological attractions. You will visit the Sacred Valley of the Incas and finally experience the most anticipated day at Machu Picchu.

The journey is completed with unique experiences in the Andes: the hike to Humantay Lake and the excursion to Rainbow Mountain. A comprehensive experience that combines culture, adventure and nature.
